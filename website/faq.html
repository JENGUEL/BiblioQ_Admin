Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does BiblioQ support?

BiblioQ v3.0 runs on Windows 10 and 11 (64-bit).

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. Library and attendance data is stored locally in SQLite on your PC.

How do I get a license?

Contact license@biblioq.com for school licensing.

How do updates work?

BiblioQ checks for updates automatically and can download and install new versions from within the app.
